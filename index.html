Pioneering Mindful Gaming for Personal Growth

Luovanori creates transformative gaming experiences that turn inner reflection into tangible growth. We believe that personal development becomes sustainable when it's engaging, visible, and deeply meaningful.

Our flagship experience, Unfolding Quest, transforms your inner voice into quests and cultivates your personal growth through an immersive garden simulation. By combining mindful reflection with engaging gameplay, we make self-discovery a journey worth taking every day.

Our Design Philosophy

1

Mindful Coaching

Inspired by June Kim's coaching questions designed for effective peer coaching and minimizing harm. These prompts guide gentle self-reflection—examining commitments, understanding outcomes, and planning meaningful next steps.

2

Generative Process

Based on Christopher Alexander's generative sequences, where each step naturally unfolds from the previous one. Your journey through our 7-step process creates a living structure—each action building upon the last to form a coherent whole.

3

Human-Centered Motivation

Using Yu-Kai Chou's Octalysis Framework, we design experiences that tap into positive, intrinsic motivations. Achievement, creativity, ownership, and social connection drive engagement that feels natural rather than forced.

Key Features

Personal Garden

Your private sanctuary where every quest completed, every insight gained, becomes part of a living, growing garden that reflects your unique journey.

AI Guide Companion

A gentle, non-judgmental AI companion that helps transform vague thoughts into clear intentions through thoughtful questions and timely encouragement.

Shared Forest

Connect with a community by sharing mature trees from your garden. See how others grow, leave encouraging messages, and find inspiration.

Growth Journal

Automatically captures your insights, the trees you've planted, and the paths you've walked, creating a beautiful visual history of your transformation.
